Frequently Asked Questions

What devices are supported?

PlateTag 66 is available for iOS devices running iOS 12.0 or later. We're working on an Android version coming soon!

Does it work offline?

Yes! PlateTag 66 works perfectly without an internet connection, so you can play anywhere your adventures take you.

Is it suitable for young children?

Absolutely! The app is designed for all ages with intuitive controls and family-friendly content that everyone can enjoy.

How does GPS tracking work?

The app uses your device's GPS to automatically detect your location and suggest nearby states. GPS usage is optional and can be disabled in settings.

Can I use it for multiple trips?

Yes! You can organize and track multiple trips, making it perfect for families who love to travel and explore new places.

What's included in the subscription?

All subscription tiers include access to all 50 states, beautiful murals, educational content, trip tracking, and offline mode.
